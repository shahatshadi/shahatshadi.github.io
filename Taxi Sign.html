LiDAR Taxi Sign

Sensing & Embedded Systems Project

Design of an New York City style taxi sign that houses two LiDARs to collect road data to advance autonomous driving technology in the North East

Pressure Clamp Mount

This mount was designed to house a pressure clamp that is able to hold down the taxi sign even against the strongest winds.

LiDAR Mount

This liDAR mount mounts onto the side of the taxi sign. The liDARs are wired through the side of the frame and connect to a PCB inside the taxi sign. Data gathered from the liDARs is displayed directly on the screen.

Embedded Control
